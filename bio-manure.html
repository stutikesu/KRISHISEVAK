Bio Manure Guidance

Learn how bio manure can revolutionize sustainable farming!

Advantages of Bio Manure

  • Improves soil fertility and texture naturally.
  • Reduces the dependency on chemical fertilizers.
  • Enhances the water retention capacity of soil.
  • Environmentally friendly and sustainable.
  • Reduces the overall cost of farming.

How to Make Bio Manure

Follow these steps to create your own bio manure:

  1. Collect organic waste such as vegetable peels, dry leaves, and food scraps.
  2. Dig a compost pit or use a large container with ventilation.
  3. Layer the organic waste and cow dung alternately in the pit.
  4. Sprinkle water to keep the mixture moist (not soggy).
  5. Turn the mixture weekly to ensure even decomposition.
  6. After 6-8 weeks, the bio manure will be ready to use.
