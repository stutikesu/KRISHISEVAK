The Role of Engineers in Krishiseva

At Krishiseva, engineers play a vital role in ensuring the smooth operation of our smart agricultural platform. They are responsible for handling technical issues related to our modules and ensuring that each farmer receives the best possible support.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Handle all technical issues related to the Krishiseva modules, sensors, and devices.
  • Communicate with farmers in a positive and clear manner to ensure they understand and are comfortable using the platform.
  • Provide technical assistance and troubleshoot any issues farmers face with devices or the Krishiseva website/app.
  • Offer guidance that helps farmers increase their usage of Krishiseva products and, in turn, improve their productivity.
  • Contribute to boosting product sales by building trust and rapport with farmers, enhancing their experience with Krishiseva.

Requirements for the Role:

  • Excellent Communication Skills: Ability to clearly communicate with farmers and explain technical concepts in a way that is easy to understand.
  • Great Convincing Power: Ability to build relationships and convince farmers of the value Krishiseva brings to their work.
  • Strong Technical Skills: Expertise in handling sensors, devices, and troubleshooting technical issues related to agricultural modules.
  • Experience with Websites and Apps: Good knowledge of navigating and operating the Krishiseva website and mobile app for seamless support.

Only those people who have above desired skills.
